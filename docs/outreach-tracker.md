## Where to find 20 programs

- Your own network first — your old teams, coaches you know, teammates now coaching. **Warm beats cold every time.**
- Local travel orgs (Google "[your city] travel baseball" / Perfect Game / GameChanger team listings).
- Nearby high school + middle school programs (school athletics pages list coach emails).
- Instagram: search your area's travel-org accounts; the coach usually DMs back faster than email.

## Weekly rhythm

- **Mon:** send/queue this week's outreach.
- **Wed–Thu:** follow up with non-responders (value-add email, not "just checking in").
- **Fri:** check in with any active team — are players logging? What's confusing?
- Log every reply + learning in Notes. Patterns here become your next product + marketing moves.

## The only metric that matters right now

**Teams actively logging.** Not signups, not site traffic. One team that would be bummed if ArmTrack disappeared > 100 curious visitors.
