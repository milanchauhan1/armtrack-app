# ArmTrack — Content Calendar & Ready-to-Film Scripts
*Goal: you spend ~20 min, 4–5×/week, filming + posting. No scripting from scratch — it's all here. Same vertical video → TikTok + IG Reels + YouTube Shorts.*

## The 5 pillars (rotate)
1. **Stakes** (fear hooks) · 2. **Mission** (your story) · 3. **Utility** (teach) · 4. **Demo** (show the app) · 5. **Proof** (users/coaches, later)

## Filming rules
- Hook in the **first 2 seconds** or they scroll. Lead with the scary stat or a bold claim.
- You on camera > polished. Phone, good light, talk like a person.
- End ~every post with a soft CTA: "It's free, link in bio."
- Caption = restate the hook + 3–5 hashtags (#baseball #pitching #armcare #travelball #tommyjohn).

## 14 ready-to-film scripts (≈15–30s each)

**S1 — Stakes / hook**
> "1 in 4 youth pitchers are throwing through arm pain right now — and most never told anyone. Here's the 60-second habit that catches it." *(cut to app)*

**S2 — Mission**
> "I threw through elbow pain for years because nobody was tracking it. It caught up with me. So I built a free app so the next kid catches it early. Here it is."

**S3 — Utility / teach**
> "Your arm soreness is telling you something. Here's what mild vs. sharp vs. lingering actually means — and when you should NOT throw."

**S4 — Demo**
> "This is the whole app. Rate your arm, enter your throws, get a readiness score out of 10 and a call: throw, light day, or rest. Under a minute. Watch."

**S5 — Stakes / stat**
> "More than half of Tommy John surgeries are kids 15 to 19. Half of those are preventable overuse. We just don't track it. Now you can — free."

**S6 — Mission / 'I make $0'**
> "I make zero dollars from this app. No ads, no selling your data. Here's why I built it anyway." *(your story, 20s)*

**S7 — Utility**
> "Pitch counts only track GAME pitches — that's like 12% of your arm's workload. Bullpens, lessons, long toss, catch — that's the other 88%. Track all of it."

**S8 — Demo / coach angle**
> "Coaches — watch this. One screen, your whole roster's arm readiness before practice. Who's good to throw, who needs a light day, who didn't check in. Free."

**S9 — Stakes / myth-bust**
> "'No pain no gain' is how you end up needing surgery at 16. Throwing through it isn't tough — it's untracked. Here's the fix."

**S10 — Utility / quick tip**
> "Post-throwing arm care in 60 seconds: [band work / icing / log it]. Do this every outing and you'll catch the warning signs before they catch you."

**S11 — Mission / build-in-public**
> "Day [X] of building a free arm-health app as a high schooler. Today I [added / fixed]. Here's where it's at." *(show progress)*

**S12 — Demo / the score reacting**
> "Watch the score drop in real time as I crank up pain and soreness — this is exactly what the app does when your arm's telling you to back off."

**S13 — Stakes / parent-targeted**
> "Parents: if your kid pitches and isn't tracking their arm, this is the free 60-second habit that could save their season. And it's actually free."

**S14 — Proof (once you have it)**
> "[Coach name]'s whole travel team is on ArmTrack now. Here's what he said." *(screenshot / clip)*
